Meet Marcus Ho

Welcome to my wild corner of the internet! I’m Marcus Ho, a passionate speedcuber, Latin enthusiast, and lover of all things ancient Rome. If you’re looking for a place where quirky interests collide, you’ve hit the jackpot!

Here, I dive deep into my eclectic passions, sharing everything from the art of solving Rubik's cubes faster than you can say 'supercalifragilisticexpialidocious' to the beauty of Latin phrases that make you sound smarter at parties. Spoiler alert: they won’t!

Join me on this rollercoaster ride of knowledge, laughter, and maybe a few eye-rolls as I blog about my adventures in speedcubing, the significance of Latin in today’s world, and the ancient Roman stuff that makes history feel like a blockbuster movie.
